**Ep. 244 — Reviving Our Passion for Android Development**
*Fragmented Podcast · Don Felker & Kaushik Gopal · 10 min · 2023-05-10*

Don and Kaushik announce that Fragmented is returning to its roots as a pure Android developer podcast. Both hosts independently found themselves doing deep Android work again — Kaushik through a subsidiary at Instacart building on Android-powered smart cards, Don joining a team behind an app with 100M+ installs — and realized their best conversations were happening off-mic, talking Android.

Going forward, expect two episodes per month on the second and fourth Monday. The podcast is also getting a visual and audio rebrand: new artwork and new music from their longtime producer Blueprint, while keeping the same format and tone.

**Why it's worth your time:** If you dropped off during the quieter years, this is the re-entry point. Two hosts actively writing Android code daily means the content should stay grounded and current.
